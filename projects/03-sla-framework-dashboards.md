# SLA Framework & Delivery Performance Dashboards

**Role:** Sr. Site Reliability Engineer, Oracle America, Inc.  
**Timeline:** Mar 2024 – Present  
**Tools:** Power BI, SQL, Python, Oracle RDBMS, Jira

---

## The Problem

The team had no formal SLAs, no consistent delivery visibility, and no structured way to identify SLA risk before it became a problem. Delivery status was communicated ad hoc. There was no way to answer basic questions — which project types run longest, which engineers are carrying the most risk, where are we trending toward a miss — without manual aggregation.

---

## What I Did

### SLA Definition

- Defined and published team SLAs from scratch, establishing measurable delivery commitments the team could be accountable to
- Standardized KPI definitions and reporting cadence so all stakeholders were working from the same baseline

### Dashboard Build

- Built Power BI dashboards providing the **first measurable view of delivery performance** for the team
- Achieved approximately **90% project tracking coverage** (gaps limited to internal and client-managed environments outside visibility)
- Enabled **drill-down cycle-time analytics** by project type, client, and engineer — making it possible to pinpoint SLA risk and focus improvement effort on the highest-impact work

### Process & Workflow Improvements

- Standardized Jira/Kanban workflow and WIP limits to reduce in-flight congestion and improve throughput
- Reduced ad hoc status requests by giving PMs and TPMs a single reliable source of truth for prerequisites and progress
- Mentored junior engineers on new workflows and incident handling patterns

---

## Impact

- Gave leadership the **first quantified, drill-capable view** of delivery performance across the team
- Shifted team status conversations from reactive and ad hoc to structured and data-driven
- Created a scalable reporting foundation that can absorb new project types and clients without rebuilding the underlying model
- Improved delivery predictability by making SLA risk visible earlier in the process

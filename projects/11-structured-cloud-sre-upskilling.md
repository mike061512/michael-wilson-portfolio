---

## Approach

The roadmap is built around a specific learning philosophy rather than a course catalog:

**Build, break, fix, explain.**  
Every topic is approached by building something, intentionally breaking it, recovering it, and documenting the reasoning behind the fix. This mirrors how SRE work actually develops expertise — through failure modes, debugging, and design tradeoffs — rather than through successful deployments alone.

**Depth over breadth.**  
Each phase is completed before moving to the next. Partial understanding of many tools is less useful than solid understanding of fewer.

**AI as review aid, not first attempt.**  
AI tools are used to review work, debug issues, and explain concepts after a first attempt — not to generate the first attempt. This preserves actual skill development rather than outsourcing understanding.

**Sustained consistency over sprints.**  
Learning is distributed across a limited weekly time budget rather than concentrated in intensive bursts that fade. The goal is durable capability, not certification prep.
